About Malitbog

Learn about our rich history, culture, and community

Our History

Malitbog Municipality was established in 1932 after separating from its mother municipality. The name "Malitbog" is derived from a local term that refers to the turbulent waters of the river that flows through the town.

In its early years, the municipality was primarily an agricultural community, with farming and fishing as the main sources of livelihood. Throughout the decades, Malitbog has transformed from a simple rural town into a progressive municipality while preserving its rich cultural heritage.

The municipality has weathered various challenges, including natural disasters and economic downturns, but has consistently shown resilience and determination to move forward. Today, Malitbog stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of its people and their commitment to community development.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide efficient and effective public service delivery for inclusive growth and sustainable development. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and participatory governance to ensure that community needs are addressed effectively.

Our Vision

A progressive, resilient, and sustainable community with empowered citizens enjoying a high quality of life. We envision Malitbog as a model municipality that balances economic development with environmental protection while nurturing its cultural identity.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold honesty, fairness, and ethical conduct in all our actions and decisions.

Transparency

We ensure open access to information and clear communication of governance processes.

Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions and are answerable to the public we serve.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in public service delivery and governance.

Citizen Participation

We value community engagement and collaborative decision-making processes.
