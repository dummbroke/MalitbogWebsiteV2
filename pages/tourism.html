Visitor Information

Plan your visit to Malitbog with these helpful tips and information to make the most of your experience.

Getting Here

You can commute from Limketkai Center, National Highway, Cagayan de Oro, 9000 Misamis Oriental using a bus. The bus will take you directly to the Malitbog Public Market, Malitbog, Bukidnon. The journey takes approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes by bus. If you're traveling by car or motorcycle, you can arrive earlier in just about 1 hour.

View Routes

Where to Stay

Many tourists choose to set up camp at Golda's Place, one of the most famous tourist spots in Malitbog. This mountain ridge offers breathtaking views and a serene campground setting perfect for overnight stays. For those seeking more traditional accommodations, there are also local homestays available in the Poblacion area.

Find Accommodations

Local Cuisine

Malitbog offers several restaurants and cafes where you can enjoy delicious local meals. From traditional Filipino dishes to fresh seafood specialties, there's something for every palate. Don't miss the opportunity to try our local delicacies, including coconut-based dishes and traditional desserts.
